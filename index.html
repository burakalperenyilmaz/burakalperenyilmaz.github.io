Privacy minded by design

Focus data is sensitive. Sway treats it that way.

Sway is designed to minimize sensitive data sharing and avoid sending raw screen time details to AI. Coaching uses limited context and the goals you choose.

This is a product principle, not a legal shortcut. See the Privacy Policy before launch for the full details.
Limited coaching context AI guidance should be based on the minimum useful context, not a full private usage history.
No shame based tracking The product avoids guilt heavy language and focuses on next actions.
User controlled routines Suggestions help users adjust routines, but the user stays in control.
FAQ

Clear answers before launch.

Sway is still being prepared for release. These answers describe the intended product direction.

Is Sway a blocker app?

Sway is primarily a focus coach and routine system. Blocking or focus protections may support the experience, but the product is not built around punishment.

How does Sway use AI?

Sway uses AI to generate practical coaching suggestions, starter plans, routine adjustments, and simple explanations that help users take the next focus step.

Does Sway send my screen time data to AI?

Sway is designed to avoid sending raw screen time details to AI. Coaching should rely on limited context and the goals or choices provided by the user.

Who is Sway for?

Sway is for students, makers, developers, remote workers, and anyone trying to rebuild focus routines without pressure.

Is Sway available on the App Store?
