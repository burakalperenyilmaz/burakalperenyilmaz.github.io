Privacy Policy

Launch ready draft. Review before App Store release if needed.

Overview

Sway Focus is designed to help users build focus routines, manage focus sessions, and receive AI guided coaching suggestions. Focus related data can be sensitive, so the product is designed to minimize unnecessary data sharing.

Information you provide

Sway may use information you choose to provide, such as focus goals, routine preferences, selected triggers, and onboarding answers, to personalize the experience.

Screen time and device context

Sway is designed to avoid sending raw screen time details to AI services. Coaching should rely on limited context, user selected goals, and app generated summaries where appropriate.

AI coaching

Sway may use AI services to generate coaching text, plans, and routine suggestions. The app is designed to share only the minimum useful context for helpful coaching.

Analytics

Sway may use privacy conscious analytics to understand app stability, feature usage, and product performance. Analytics should not be used to sell personal data.

Purchases

If Sway offers subscriptions or in app purchases, payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. Sway does not directly process payment card information.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@swayfocus.com.
